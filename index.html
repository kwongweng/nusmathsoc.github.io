The NUS Mathematical Society is a student organization dedicated to advancing the study and appreciation of mathematics at the National University of Singapore. We serve as a bridge between students, faculty, and the broader mathematical community.

Whether you're a mathematics major, minor, or simply passionate about the subject, our society welcomes all who share an interest in the beauty and power of mathematical thinking.
